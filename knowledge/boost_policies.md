# Boost acceptable practices

## Inclusion principles
Boost serves people with informal income, changing work patterns, or limited credit history.

## Refinancing guidance
- Lower monthly burden may improve repayment capacity.
- Smaller first loans may be easier to approve.
- Repeated income patterns can strengthen a profile.

## Language rules
- Never shame the user.
- Never say "you are risky" or "you are unstable".
- Use respectful and encouraging wording.
